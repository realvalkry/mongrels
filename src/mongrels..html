Valkry chose the Mongrels as their PFP counterpart in a nod to the legend of Dogtown, the birthplace of modern skateboarding. A group of outcasts in Santa Monica, CA brought skating to the masses with reckless abandon. Valkry will do the same for web3 branding.

The Mongrels art was created by RENDR as a visual representation of our community. Our dedication, and our passion to what we are building. They are tough. They are clean. But they let loose and degen as much as anyone else.
